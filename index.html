ASAssassins

Rules:

  1. This game will be played over the course of many days.
  2. In order to win, you must eliminate the target assigned to you.
  3. Each of you will be assigned an Assassin number that you are to relinguish to your killer in the event that you are slayed.
  4. In order to kill someone, you must slice from shoulder to shoulder (ON THE BACK!) with a plastic spoon obtained from any of the dining halls.
    (There will be some left in the XLounge as well for those who can't frequent the dining halls)
  5. Once you've finished off your target, report their code to jea2161@columbia.edu with subject "Kill Code"for your next target.
  6. There are designated Safe Spaces below that killing is not allowed.
  7. In the event that a discrepancy arises, please contact jea2161@columbia.edu with the subject "Kill Confusion" to be resolve the situation.
  8. You can not kill your subject if they're in the midst of eating.
  9. Please exercise rational judgement in your practices. (Don't be wild)
  10. If no one has made a kill within 48 hours, a state of anarchy will be instated.
  11. In the state of anarchy, all assassins are free to kill anyone else who remains in the game.
